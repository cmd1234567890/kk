place

  • Summer Palace

    The Summer Palace, formerly known as the Qingyi Garden, is a vast imperial garden located in the western suburbs of Beijing. Covering an area of 3.009 square kilometers, it boasts a quarter of its territory dedicated to water, creating a scenic landscape that resembles the West Lake in Hangzhou.

  • Forbidden City

    The Forbidden City, located in the center of Beijing, is a testament to the grandeur and majesty of the imperial era. Once the exclusive residence of the emperors of the Ming and Qing dynasties, it exudes an air of regality and solemnity.
